About the Event

Join us for the annual Summer Music Festival in beautiful downtown Prince George! This year's lineup features amazing local bands and special guest performers from across BC.

Featured Acts:

  • Northern Lights Band
  • Prince George Symphony
  • Local Folk Trio

Food trucks and local vendors will be on site. Free admission for children under 12.
